Welcome to FracTect!

This software is used to classify and detect wrist fractures in X-rays!
It has two neural networks, one for classifying an X-ray into Fractured or Not Fractured,
and the other to identify in an X-ray where a fracture may be.
Head over to the FracTect tab to get started!
Documentation and the source code can be found in their respective tabs.
For more information, or to contact me, go to the About tab.
